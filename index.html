Dr. Linchao Zhu (朱林超) is currently an Assistant Professor with the College of Computer Science at Zhejiang University. Before that, he was a Lecturer at the ReLER lab, University of Technology Sydney. His research focus includes foundation models, multi-modal learning, and AI for scientific discovery. He received his Ph.D. degree in University of Technology Sydney, advised by Prof. Yi Yang. He graduated from Zhejiang University with a bachelor’s degree.
